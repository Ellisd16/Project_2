Here with Charles Pinks,
the reason we exist is the pursuit of beauty. To create sound that draws you in and pulls at the deepest parts of us, a medium which we are inspired by, one that is lasting and rooted in tradition. A strong focus on the purity of craft, the perfection of every instrument we create being the goal. From the form and pattern, to the color of our varnish, and most importantly the final sound; all done with a keen eye and a vision of beauty.
